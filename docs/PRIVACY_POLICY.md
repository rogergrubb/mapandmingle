# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

Map Mingle ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, profile photo, bio, age, and gender
- **Profile Information**: Interests, looking for preferences, activity intent
- **Content**: Pins, posts, comments, and messages you create
- **Location Data**: Your location when you choose to share it

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Features used, time spent, interactions with other users
- **Location Data**: Approximate location for nearby features (with permission)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Map Mingle service
- Connect you with other users based on location and interests
- Send notifications about waves, messages, and nearby activity
- Improve and personalize your experience
- Ensure safety and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share information:
- **With Other Users**: Profile information, pins, and posts are visible to other users based on your privacy settings
- **Service Providers**: Companies that help us operate (hosting, analytics)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger or acquisition

## Your Privacy Controls

You can:
- **Adjust Visibility**: Control who can see your profile and location
- **Ghost Mode**: Browse without being visible to others (Premium)
- **Block Users**: Prevent specific users from contacting you
- **Delete Account**: Remove your account and associated data
- **Export Data**: Request a copy of your data

## Location Data

- Location is only collected when you grant permission
- You can disable location access in your device settings
- Location is used to show nearby users, pins, and events
- We do not continuously track your location

## Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS)
- Secure data storage
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls for our team

## Data Retention

- We retain your data while your account is active
- After account deletion, data is removed within 30 days
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance

## Children's Privacy

Map Mingle is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

## International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using Map Mingle, you consent to this transfer.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
- Say no to the sale of personal information
- Access personal information
- Request deletion of personal information
- Equal service and price

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@mapmingle.app.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU residents have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Object to processing
- Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@mapmingle.app.

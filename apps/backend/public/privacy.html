Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 28, 2024

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve Map Mingle's features
  • Connect you with nearby users
  • Send notifications about matches and messages
  • Ensure safety and prevent abuse
  • Process payments for Premium subscriptions
  • Respond to support requests

3. Location Data

Location is core to Map Mingle. We collect location when:

  • You open the map to see nearby activity
  • You create a pin or event
  • You share your location with another user

You can disable location permissions in your device settings, but this will limit app functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Other users: Your public profile and pins
  • Service providers: Hosting, payments, analytics
  • Legal authorities: When required by law

5. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information
  • Delete: Delete your account and data
  • Withdraw consent: Opt out of optional data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us or use the settings in the app.

7. Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. After deletion:

  • Profile data is deleted within 30 days
  • Messages may be retained for other participants
  • Some data may be kept for legal compliance

8. Children's Privacy

Map Mingle is not for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us.

9. Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services:

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Expo: Push notifications
  • Cloud hosting: Data storage

These services have their own privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
